Why the interest in Iceland?

My first exposure to the beauty of Iceland came from the 2013 movie The Secret Life of Walter Mitty. After seeing that movie I mentally added Iceland to my travel bucket list. In July 2021 I spent a week driving around the ring road that circles the island and seeing as many of the amazing natural wonders as I could. Honestly, I truly had no idea how wonderful and magical this country was until I arrived. Beyond the amazing scenary, Icelanders are very friendly and welcoming. They also teach English in schools so it makes it easy to get around.
